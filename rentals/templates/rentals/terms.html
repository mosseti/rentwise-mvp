Terms Of Use

RentWise helps users discover caretaker-managed rental listings. Listings are provided by caretakers or admins and should be confirmed before any payment is made.

Important Rental Safety

Do not send deposits before viewing a unit, confirming the caretaker, and receiving proper payment instructions. RentWise may remove suspicious listings or accounts.

Caretaker Responsibility

Caretakers must provide accurate prices, photos, availability, phone contacts, and building information. Fake or misleading listings can lead to suspension.
