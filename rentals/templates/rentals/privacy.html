Privacy

Privacy Policy

RentWise collects only the information needed to run the rental marketplace: account details, phone contacts, listings, images, searches, assistant messages, and viewing requests.

We use this data to show listings, connect users with caretakers, improve search, prevent fake listings, and manage platform safety.

Public Information

Building names, locations, uploaded photos, available unit prices, and caretaker contact details may be visible to house seekers when a listing is approved and published.
